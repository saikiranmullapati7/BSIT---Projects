About Masque

Masque Restaurant is an ingredient‑driven, fine‑dining space in Mumbai that reimagines modern Indian cuisine through a seasonal, chef‑led tasting menu. It is widely regarded as one of India’s most innovative restaurants, blending deep regional roots with contemporary technique and design.

Who we are

Masque was founded in 2016 by entrepreneur Aditi Dugar and Chef Prateek Sadhu, and today it is led by Head Chef Varun Totlani. The restaurant is set inside one of Mumbai’s historic textile mills, turning the city’s industrial heritage into a backdrop for a refined, immersive dining experience.

Our philosophy

Masque focuses on seasonal, regional produce, sourcing ingredients from its own farm, foraging trips, and a network of small‑scale farmers across India. The menu changes with the seasons and is built around a 10‑course tasting format, making it one of the first restaurants in India to offer chef’s tasting‑menu‑only fine dining.

Innovation and creativity

The kitchen treats the restaurant as a living laboratory, where classic Indian dishes are deconstructed and reimagined using modern techniques and global influences. The Masque Lab, a dedicated research space, formalizes this spirit of experimentation, exploring how ingredients age, respond to different flavors, and connect different food cultures.

Experience and hospitality

Masque aims to create a curated, multi‑sensory journey—where food, service, and the mill‑land setting work together to tell the story of India’s diverse culinary landscape. The team combines a rigorous approach to technique with warm, attentive hospitality, positioning the restaurant as both a destination for locals and a benchmark for modern Indian fine dining on the global stage.
